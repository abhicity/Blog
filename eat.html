1947 Restaurant

If you are looking for pure vegetarian restaurant this is right place for you. 1947 restaurant is well know for veg restaurant for North Indian and Chinese food menu. Talking about our experience it was so good with 1947, We had opt for buffet service and visited this place at afternoon for lunch. It was crowded and buffet wasn’t timely refilled we had to wait wait and wait, Taste was also okay okay We dint had good experience with staffs aswell. And of course buffet has large spread with varieties of food. Starting from soup, salads , Chinese, main courses, chats , starters and desserts. May be we experienced as such bcz of crowd not sure, I suggest to order food from menu rather going to buffet service.





Desi Masala North Indian Restaurant in Banaswadi

Desi masala provides excellent quality unlimited food for 220rs and is completely worth it.
They provide a lemon drink which is excellent along with 3 types of starters which is also unlimited. all the sides are available in the form of buffet.
veg soup, tomato soup tasted average.
sweets such as halwa and jamoon is unlimited.
rice items such as pulav, fried rice, rice and sambar, curd rice are also available. The rotis were served hot directly from the server.
we had multiple variety of chat plates and finished it off with multiple scoops of vanilla ice cream. even the washroom was well sanitised and clean.
overall conclusion is that the food is definitely worth the price and i recommend to try it once.
